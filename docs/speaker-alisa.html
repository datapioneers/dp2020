Speaker Details

Alisa Krstova

Data Scientist @ Airbus Defence and Space

Alisa is passionate about uncovering knowledge hidden in large amounts of data. In her role at Airbus, she focuses on exploring how AI and machine learning can transform and optimize the ways of working and help engineers make better informed decisions. She attended the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje from 2014 to 2018, where she also worked as a part-time teaching assistant. Ambivert, curious, she believes that with a cup of good coffee anything is possible.

Transforming Big Data into Linked Data for Satellite Engineering

Building satellites is complex. From design to testing and operations, the process generates massive datasets from heterogeneous sources. Find out how we use linked data and knowledge graphs to simplify the navigation through such complex data and pave the way to a smart digital assistant.

Data Pioneers is a conference on data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence. It's organised for data scientists, data engineers, data science managers and all other data science enthusiasts. Interested? Then join us! We are looking forward to seeing you at the conference.
