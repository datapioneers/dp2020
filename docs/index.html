Data Pioneers Night

November 19th 2020, 20:15, Online

About The Event

No TV tonight, join us for an evening of data fun! Prepare your snacks & drinks, get comfy, and tune in at 20:15!

This year we decided to host a virtual Data Pioneers night. One evening with exciting short talks on data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Where

Online

When

Thursday, 19 November 2020

Speakers

Data Pioneers 2020 tries something new—the format is less formal, slides without text are a rule. The premise for the speakers is "Imagine that you are in a bar, there is cool music running in the back and you talk to a person that you just got to know. Both of you work with data, and you now explain the cool project that you are working on." Cheers!
